Eugenio Landi


About me:

I am currently a short term researcher and lecturer at the Eberly College of Science, Department of Mathematics, Penn State University, starting in August 2022 until December 2022.

I obtained my Ph.D. at Università di Roma Tre under the supervision of professor Domenico Fiorenza.
My research interests are algebraic topology and higher category theory.
For my Ph.D. thesis, titled "Equivariant localization methods, orientations and modularity", I worked on orientations, T-duality, rational homotopy theory, TQFTs and equivariant cohomology.
